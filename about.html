About this site

Finding a good tutor in Adelaide shouldn't be guesswork.

Adelaide Tutor Reviews exists because choosing a tutor usually comes down to a Google star rating and a hopeful phone call. I wanted something better for local families, so I started writing it.

Who's behind this

I'm a local with a long-standing interest in education, and I've spent years helping family and friends find (and sometimes escape) tutors across Adelaide. After one too many conversations that started with "do you know anyone good for Year 11 maths?", I decided to start documenting what I found properly.

This site is a personal project. I'm not affiliated with any tutoring business, school or agency, and no tutor has ever paid to appear here or to look better than they deserve.

How I review

Every review on this site is based on first-hand contact with the tutor or centre, plus conversations with families who use them. Where possible I sit in on a real session or arrange a trial lesson, and I always speak to at least two current or recent families before publishing.

Teaching quality

Does the tutor actually teach (diagnose gaps, explain clearly, adapt on the fly) or just supervise homework?

Communication

Do parents know what's happening? Progress updates, honest feedback and responsiveness between sessions.

Value for money

Price measured against what's delivered, not just the cheapest hourly rate on the block.

Reliability

Sessions that happen when booked, consistent tutors and admin that doesn't get in the way of learning.

Each category is scored out of five, and the overall rating is my judgement call rather than a strict average, because a tutor who is brilliant at teaching but never turns up isn't a 3.5, they're a problem.

What the stars mean

A 5 means I'd recommend them without hesitation and there's a waitlist for a reason. A 4 is a strong choice with minor caveats. A 3 means it depends on your child, so read the review closely. Below that, I'd keep looking, and the review will tell you exactly why.

Independence, corrections and fairness

I don't accept payment, free lessons or gifts from tutors, and there are no affiliate links on this site. Tutors are welcome to respond to their review. If I've got something factually wrong, I'll correct it and note the correction. Opinions, though, stay mine.

A note for tutors: if you'd like your service considered for review, or you want to respond to an existing review, get in touch via the contact page. Being reviewed is free and cannot be bought.
